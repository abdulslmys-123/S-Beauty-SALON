Our Story

More than a salon —
a sanctuary

Nestled inside Kargo Mall in the heart of First Al Sheikh Zayed, S Beauty Salon was built on one belief: every woman deserves to feel extraordinary. We created a space that is warm, welcoming, and entirely focused on you.

Led by Samer and Youssef — two of Sheikh Zayed's most sought-after stylists — our team combines years of craft with a genuine passion for beauty. Clients who come once tend to come back for years.

From precise cuts and dramatic colour transformations to bridal glam and nail art, every service is delivered with skill, care, and an eye for detail that has earned us 4.9 stars and a loyal following.
